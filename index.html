Objective Nepal

Nepal is where everything began for me and my second home. It's a lifelong destination for many. I owe to the country and its people the motivations that drove me to travel for a living, to the point of making it my profession.

With Viaggia con Carlo, I specialized in Nepal , as a travel and life destination : I also dedicated the Finale for Nepal project to the people, their history, and their culture, which must be protected and supported.

NEPAL IS MORE THAN A DESTINATION

Nepal is more than a destination. It's the place that has seen all of us become expert travelers. That's why we've made it the protagonist of our destinations. But it's its spirit that has influenced the way we make Nepal trips a total experience.

NEPAL IN PREVIEW

Living the journey

I consider myself a world explorer . For me, traveling means moving, with a free and respectful spirit , to encounter places and people. It means feeling enriched by the lands I've encountered, the challenges I've faced, the goals I've achieved, and the dreams I've fulfilled. There's too much beauty to keep to myself. Whether it's Asia, Africa, Europe, or South America, I'm ready, along with my group of companions, to travel with you in a safe and organized manner.

And it won't just be a journey but a true encounter: with others, with the world, with yourself.
