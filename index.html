Which journey resembles you?

I'm Carlo and I work in the travel industry. But not alone. With a group of guides, companions, and friends, I run the tour operator Viaggia con Carlo. I design
travel experiences combined with precise logistical organization. Trips to Nepal, mountaineering, trekking, adventure and solidarity trips, but
also cultural and leisure tours: these are the trips I love to promote.
There's so much life inside these destinations, you risk becoming addicted to them. That's the beauty of traveling, of finding your place in the world, the one
that's like you: mine is definitely Nepal.
